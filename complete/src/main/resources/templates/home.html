Pay a TV Licence fine


You can pay a TV license fine online or by phone


How to pay

You will need your credit or debit card to pay for the fine

You must pay the fine x days given your notice of fine, otherwise x


Online Payment

You will need to login using your Government Gateway User ID

Alternatively, you can use your letter id, last name and post code


Other ways to pay
Call 0300 790 9901 to pay a fine in England
Call 0300 790 9980 to pay a fine in Wales
